This Nextcloud News Reader app makes it possible to synchronize feeds between Android and the Nextcloud/ownCloud News App and let's you read RSS feeds on the go.

'''Features:'''

* Offline reading
* Cache images offline
* Background synchronization
* Customizable Listview
* Mark as read while scrolling
* Dark/Light Theme
* Customizable font
* Widget
* Pull-To-Refresh
* and many more!
.
